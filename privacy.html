Privacy Policy for RunCall

Last updated: January 27, 2026

RunCall uses your Google Calendar data for one purpose only: to help you manage your availability for consultation sessions.

Data Access

We access your Google Calendar to:

  • Read your events to identify busy slots (so clients can't book you when you are busy).
  • Create new events when a client books a session.

Data Storage

We do not store your calendar details on our servers. We only store the "RunCall" appointments created through our platform.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your Google user data to any third-party AI tools or advertisers.
